BIM Coordination Workflow

Our structured BIM workflow ensures efficient coordination, accurate modeling, and smooth construction execution.

01 Design Model Review

Review architectural, structural, and MEP design models to understand project requirements.

02 BIM Modeling

Develop coordinated BIM models across ARC, STR, and MEP disciplines.

03 Clash Detection

Perform clash detection using Navisworks to identify coordination conflicts.

04 Coordination Resolution

Resolve clashes and optimize system layouts for construction feasibility.

05 Shop Drawing Extraction

Generate construction-ready drawings from the coordinated BIM model.

06 Construction Support
