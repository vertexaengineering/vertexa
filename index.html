Architectural BIM Modeling (ARC)

Architectural BIM modeling focuses on creating accurate digital representations of building layouts, spatial planning, façade elements, and architectural details. At VERTEXA Digital Engineering, we develop coordinated architectural models that support design validation, construction planning, and multidisciplinary coordination.

Our ARC modeling process ensures alignment between design intent and constructability. We integrate architectural models with structural and MEP systems to identify clashes early and ensure smooth construction execution.

Structural BIM Modeling (STR)

Structural BIM modeling focuses on developing accurate digital models of the structural framework of a building, including columns, beams, slabs, foundations, and reinforcement elements.

At VERTEXA Digital Engineering, our structural models are developed to support coordination, analysis workflows, and construction planning. Structural BIM models are integrated with architectural and MEP systems to detect clashes, optimize structural layouts, and ensure efficient site execution.
