Architectural BIM Modeling (ARC)

Architectural BIM modeling focuses on creating accurate digital representations of building layouts, spatial planning, façade elements, and architectural details. At VERTEXA Digital Engineering, we develop coordinated architectural models that support design validation, construction planning, and multidisciplinary coordination.

Our ARC modeling process ensures alignment between design intent and constructability. We integrate architectural models with structural and MEP systems to identify clashes early and ensure smooth construction execution.

Architectural Layout Modeling

Detailed BIM modeling for walls, floors, openings, and architectural layouts.

Façade Coordination

Integration of façade components within the BIM coordination environment.

Architectural Coordination View

Architectural models integrated with structural and MEP systems.

Structural BIM Modeling (STR)

Structural BIM modeling focuses on developing accurate digital models of the structural framework of a building, including columns, beams, slabs, foundations, and reinforcement elements.

At VERTEXA Digital Engineering, our structural models are developed to support coordination, analysis workflows, and construction planning. Structural BIM models are integrated with architectural and MEP systems to detect clashes, optimize structural layouts, and ensure efficient site execution.

Structural Frame Modeling

Detailed modeling of beams, columns, and slab systems.

Reinforcement Coordination

Structural modeling aligned with reinforcement and coordination requirements.

Structural Coordination

Integrated coordination between structural and MEP systems.

MEP BIM Modeling (Mechanical • Electrical • Plumbing)

MEP BIM modeling focuses on the coordination and integration of mechanical, electrical, and plumbing systems within the building environment. These systems are critical for the operational functionality of modern buildings.

At VERTEXA Digital Engineering, we develop coordinated MEP models that ensure proper routing of ducts, pipes, cable trays, and equipment while avoiding clashes with architectural and structural components.

Mechanical System Coordination

HVAC duct routing and equipment coordination within BIM models.

Electrical Routing

Cable trays, conduits, and electrical system modeling for coordination.

Plumbing System Modeling

Pipe routing and plumbing systems integrated with other disciplines.

Landscape Design & BIM Integration

At VERTEXA Digital Engineering, landscape models support site planning, grading studies, and visualization of external environments including hardscape, planting layouts, and site amenities.
